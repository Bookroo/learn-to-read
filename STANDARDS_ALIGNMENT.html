Beyond the standards

Alignment charts can't capture the parts of Bookroo that make the standards stick:

A teacher in every session

Lessons are fully scripted for a parent or tutor — what to say (read aloud), what to watch for (helper-only notes), when to wait. Delivery is 1:1 by design.

100% decodability discipline

Every word a child is asked to read is verified against the cumulative taught set by our curriculum tooling — including game distractors and book text. Word selection is screened against a children's-picture-book frequency lexicon.

Real books from Lesson 1

Three letters are enough for a first story; every book ends with comprehension questions.

Spiral review with readiness gates

Every module ends in a review lesson with an explicit parent-facing readiness check before advancing.

Named, kid-facing strategies

Finger Words, Sticky Sounds, Letter Friends, Heart Words, Brain Words — children learn how to attack words, not just which words.

Free

Every lesson, book, game, and printable.
